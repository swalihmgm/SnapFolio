Mohamed Abid Portfolio Design

This project represents a modern, high-end portfolio design for Mohamed Abid. The design focuses on "Bringing Ideas to Life with Design & Motion", featuring a dark mode aesthetic with vibrant accents.

The layout is crafted to showcase creative expertise in visuals, dynamic motion graphics, and seamless web experiences. It includes a sleek hero section, interactive elements, and a clean navigation system.
